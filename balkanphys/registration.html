Participation fees

Students

Students can choose participation fees with or without hostel accommodation. Team-building events on Saturday are not included in these prices and will be paid for separately during the event.

Participation fee without accommodation includes:

  • Admission to all events on Thursday and Friday
  • Nights out at Aquarius and Pivnica HS
  • A printed copy of the conference program and a welcome bag
  • Lunch, snacks, and coffee during breaks
  • Certificate of participation

The participation fee with accommodation includes all of the above in addition to 2 nights and breakfasts at one of the hostels we have partnered with, Hostel Franz Ferdinand and Hostel Walter. Please see Travel for more information regarding accommodations.

Faculty mentors

Registration for professors and faculty mentors attending the quiz is free of charge. Please make sure to book your accommodations separately. Under Travel we have provided a list of hotels near the Medical Faculty that you might wish to look at. Team-building events on Saturday are not included with registration and will be paid for separately.
