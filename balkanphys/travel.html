BPC will take place at the Medical Faculty at the University of Sarajevo, a 10-minute walk from the center of the city. The quiz events will be held in an auditorium located in the main building near the main entrance.

Accommodations

We are pleased to have partnered with two hostels in Sarajevo, Franz Ferdinand Hostel (Jelića 4) and Hostel Walter (Safvet-bega Bašagića 37), to offer student competitors accommodation during BPC. Each hostel can accommodate roughly 50 people. Hostel accommodation is 9 euros per person per day and includes breakfast, coffee and tea, sheets and towels, computers to use, Wi-Fi, and locker rooms. You will have a chance to select this option when you register for the competition.

Click below on a list of hotels located within walking distance from the Medical Faculty at the University of Sarajevo that you might be interested in. Please note that BPC does not book accommodations other than at the two hostels noted above. Please reserve your own hotels if you’d like to stay elsewhere!

Airports

Sarajevo International Airport is located 12 kilometers from the center of Sarajevo. It serves as a connection to many European cities and to the rest of the world through major international airline hubs. See more at airport's website. We recommend checking out WizzAir and Eurowings for cheap flights!

Tuzla International Airport is located 110 kilometers from Sarajevo. It serves as a connection for several budget airlines. Recommendations: check WizzAir for cheap flights from Vienna, Berlin, Cologne, Dortmund, Frankfurt, Eindhoven, Gothenburg, Stockholm, Malmo to Tuzla, then hop on a 2-hour bus to Sarajevo.

Mostar International Airport and Banja Luka International Airport are good options if you would like to fly Eurowings or Ryanair and see a little more of the country.

Bus stations

Sarajevo International Bus Station is Sarajevo’s main bus station and is located near the city center and the main railway station. It is the starting point for most buses to domestic as well as international destinations. Recently, Flixbus has begun operating in Sarajevo. Intercity lines operate every day to Sarajevo from Banja Luka, Bihać, Fojnica, Jajce, Međugorje, Travnik, Tuzla, Visoko, Zenica, and other cities in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Visa Information

Citizens from most countries do not require a visa to travel to BiH. If you do require a visa, we will provide you with an official letter of invitation from the medical faculty. Please provide all necessary information when registering so that we can properly fill out your invitation letter.

Additional visa information can be found at the website of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
